Skylines I've Never Seen: Net-Real China as Hopeium

(2025) essay / Institute of Network Cultures



A longform for the Institute of Network Cultures, psychoanalyzing my obsession with China and the Chinese internet. Alongside making of my two works shown at Current Affairs, You Were Farming Rice, Now You're Farming Clicks and Xiangkai Network Experience Co., Ltd., I was actively researching and writing about Chinese network cultures and their surfacing on the Western internet. Deeply immersed in the topic, I have never asked myself the question of why am I so enthralled by it. This essay is an attempt at analyzing the online side of The Chinese Century, and most importantly—my place within it.
